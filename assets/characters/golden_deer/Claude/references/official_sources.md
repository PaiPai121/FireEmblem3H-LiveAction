- Brown curly / wavy hair, green eyes, tan skin.
- Relaxed smile with tactical, unreadable intent.
- Golden Deer academy uniform with yellow/gold accents.
- Bow / strap / archer-adventurer identity where appropriate.
- Student leader, playful but calculating.
