# Claude Fidelity Review

Reviewed asset: `academy/selected.png`

## Current Strengths

- Brown curls, green eyes, warm tan complexion, and yellow Golden Deer palette read well.
- Expression has some playful intelligence.
- Academy setting and uniform are coherent.

## Fidelity Gaps

- Hair is close but needs a more distinctive Claude silhouette.
- Costume is still a generic black academy uniform with yellow cape, not enough game-specific Golden Deer structure.
- Archer / schemer identity is understated.
- Needs more sly tactical confidence and less straightforward charming student portrait.

## Required Fixes

- Make yellow/gold Golden Deer elements more clearly tied to the official academy design.
- Include a practical strap or bow-adjacent detail without making it a battle scene.
- Expression should be relaxed, amused, and calculating.
- Avoid generic prince styling or overly formal noble costume.

## Priority

Very high. Claude is one of the three route identity anchors.
