## Visual Anchors To Extract

- Pale silver-white hair, academy-era compact style.
- Violet eyes and composed, severe expression.
- Black Eagles academy uniform with red, black, white, and gold structure.
- Imperial red accents, not generic red cloak only.
- Young student leader, not adult emperor in academy version.
