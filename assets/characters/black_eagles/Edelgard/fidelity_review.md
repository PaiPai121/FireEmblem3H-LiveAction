# Edelgard Fidelity Review

Reviewed asset: `academy/selected.png`

## Current Strengths

- Silver-white hair, violet eyes, black-red academy palette, and serious expression are strong.
- Age reads closer to academy era than earlier overaged versions.
- Live-action realism and cloth quality are good.

## Fidelity Gaps

- Hair length and shape are too loose and long for academy Edelgard.
- Uniform is plausible but not faithful enough to the specific Black Eagles academy design.
- Red cape and crest patch feel invented; the official silhouette needs clearer white/red/black structure.
- Needs more imperial discipline and less generic noble school portrait.

## Required Fixes

- Shorten and structure the silver-white hair closer to academy Edelgard.
- Make the uniform more game-faithful: black student jacket, red imperial accents, white inner collar/cravat area, gold trim.
- Keep her youthful but severe; avoid adult queen or emperor styling.
- Avoid long romantic hair, crown, armor, or heavy commander presence.

## Priority

Very high. Edelgard is one of the three route identity anchors.
