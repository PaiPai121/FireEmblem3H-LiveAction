# Sothis Dream Candidates Reference V1 Notes

## Candidate 01

Path: `candidate_01.png`

Status: candidate, not selected.

Strengths:

- Side hair buns, pointed ears, gold headpiece, and throne setting are much closer to official Sothis.
- Blue, purple, white, pink, and gold costume blocks are clearer than the legacy EP01 candidate.
- Expression is quiet and detached enough for EP01.

Issues:

- Shoulder exposure and pose are a little too mature / glamorous.
- The official sword is not included; acceptable for portrait use, but less useful for full design reference.
- Needs a slightly younger, stranger, more ancient consciousness feel in the next pass.

Recommendation:

Use as a strong direction reference. Generate more candidates with less glamour and more restrained dream presence before selecting.

## Candidate 02

Path: `candidate_02.png`

## Candidate 03

Path: `candidate_03.png`

Status: candidate, not selected.

Strengths:

- Much cuter and softer than candidate 02, with cleaner youthful skin and a more appealing Sothis-like face.
- Keeps the official throne pose, green side buns, gold headpiece with tassels, sword silhouette, navy robe, white drapes, pink ribbons, and gold costume accents.
- Better addresses the viewer feedback that Sothis was not cute enough.

Issues:

- The pose follows the official reference more closely, so leg exposure is stronger than candidate 02.
- Ancient / detached consciousness is slightly reduced because the expression is warmer and sweeter.
- Needs final review against the EP01 tone before replacing the current selected image.

Recommendation:

Use candidate 03 as the current best "cute and clean skin" Sothis direction. If EP01 needs a more mysterious tone, generate candidate 04 by blending candidate 03's face and skin with candidate 02's more restrained body language.
