# Sothis Fidelity Review

Reviewed asset: `dream/legacy_ep01/candidate_03.png`

## Current Strengths

- Green hair, pointed ears, ornate gold headpiece, and purple-gold costume are recognizable.
- Youthful face and quiet expression fit EP01.
- Overall divine fantasy tone is strong.

## Fidelity Gaps

- Hair shape is too naturalistic and lacks the exact side-bun / braided silhouette from the game.
- Costume is ornate but not yet specifically Sothis; it reads like a generic elven princess.
- Needs stronger ancient consciousness in expression, less innocent child portrait.
- Blue/purple/black costume blocks should be clearer.

## Required Fixes

- Push the gold headpiece and forehead ornament closer to official silhouette.
- Add distinctive side hair masses while keeping live-action realism.
- Keep purple/navy/gold robe structure visible.
- Expression should be curious, old, and still, not cute or playful.

## Priority

Very high. Sothis defines EP01's visual identity.
