## Visual Anchors To Extract

- Very long green hair with distinctive side buns / braided side masses.
- Pointed ears.
- Gold headpiece and forehead ornament.
- Purple, navy, black, and gold divine costume.
- Small youthful body with ancient, detached expression.
- Dreamlike throne-space presence, not a cheerful child.
