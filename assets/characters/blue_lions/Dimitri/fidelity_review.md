# Dimitri Fidelity Review

Reviewed asset: `academy/selected.png`

## Current Strengths

- Blond hair, blue eyes, blue cape, and princely seriousness are readable.
- Age and face are appropriate for academy-era Dimitri.
- Costume has strong Blue Lions color identity.

## Fidelity Gaps

- The large blue cape dominates and makes him look more like a fantasy prince than an academy student.
- Uniform needs more specific academy jacket structure and less generic royal costume.
- Hair shape is plausible but should be more recognizably Dimitri.
- Should imply polite restraint with inner tension, not only heroic nobility.

## Required Fixes

- Keep blue accents but reduce cape dominance for academy version.
- Add clearer white/blue/black academy uniform structure with gold trim.
- Hair should remain blond and slightly tousled, but more game-faithful.
- Avoid king, knight commander, armor, or war veteran styling.

## Priority

Very high. Dimitri is one of the three route identity anchors.
