## Visual Anchors To Extract

- Blond hair, blue eyes, polite prince-like academy presence.
- Blue Lions academy uniform with blue, black, white, and gold structure.
- Upright posture, restrained nobility, hidden tension.
- Academy version should not look battle-hardened.
