## Visual Anchors To Extract

- Teal / dark green hair, slightly messy, sharp silhouette.
- Calm, unreadable mercenary-professor expression.
- Black professor outfit with structured long coat.
- Dark leather / cloth layers, high collar, belts and metal details.
- Subtle fantasy officer / mercenary silhouette, not a generic wizard robe.
