# Byleth Fidelity Review

Reviewed asset: `professor/selected.png`

## Current Strengths

- Teal hair and calm expression read clearly.
- Black costume and monastery setting fit the professor era.
- The image has strong live-action realism.

## Fidelity Gaps

- Costume reads as a generic black fantasy professor robe rather than Byleth's specific long-coat mercenary/professor design.
- Chest pendant and belt ornament are invented and do not clearly match the game silhouette.
- Hair silhouette is close, but should be more intentionally Byleth-like rather than loose medieval styling.
- Needs more mercenary restraint; current styling is slightly too polished.

## Required Fixes

- Emphasize structured black long coat, layered straps, fitted torso, and practical mercenary details.
- Keep teal hair but make the shape sharper and less romantic.
- Avoid large invented magical pendants, oversized cloaks, or generic mage robes.
- Keep expression blank, observant, and emotionally restrained.

## Priority

High. Byleth appears in EP01 and is a project identity anchor.
