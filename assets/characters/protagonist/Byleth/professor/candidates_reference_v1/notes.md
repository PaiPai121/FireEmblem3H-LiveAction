# Byleth Professor Candidates Reference V1 Notes

## Candidate 01

Path: `candidate_01.png`

Status: candidate, not selected.

Strengths:

- Much closer to official Byleth costume silhouette than the previous `selected.png`.
- Shoulder armor-like panels, gloves/bracers, fitted black coat, and belt structure are clearer.
- Hair is darker and sharper, closer to the official teal-black shape.
- Expression is restrained and mercenary-like.

Issues:

- Chest/waist ornaments are still partly invented and should be reduced in the next pass.
- Coat reads slightly too armored and heavy for professor-era Byleth.
- Needs a bit more of the official long split-tail coat silhouette.

Recommendation:

Use as a stronger direction reference, but generate at least 3 more candidates before replacing `selected.png`.

## Candidate 02

Path: `candidate_02.png`

Status: candidate, not selected.

Strengths:

- Less armored than candidate 01 and closer to the professor long-coat silhouette.
- Better for academy/professor scenes because the design feels more practical and understated.
- Hair remains close to the official dark teal shape.

Issues:

- Still has small invented chest ornamentation.
- Needs clearer lower coat split-tail shape if used as final.

Recommendation:

Prefer candidate 02 over candidate 01 for Byleth's professor-era base direction.

## Candidate 03

Path: `candidate_03.png`

Status: candidate, not selected.

Strengths:

- Cleaner face and softer film lighting than candidate 02; less rough and less weathered.
- Keeps the restrained professor mood, dark teal-black hair, black long coat, gloves, and Garreg Mach cloister setting.
- Useful as a skin / lighting quality reference for later Byleth passes.

Issues:

- Costume still reads as a grounded original black uniform more than a faithful Byleth outfit.
- Female Byleth-specific chest, pink cord, lace, and sword cues are mostly absent.
- Not enough of a design improvement to replace candidate 02 by itself.

Recommendation:

Use candidate 03 as the clean-skin quality reference. For final selection, generate another pass that keeps candidate 03's face quality but restores more official Byleth costume identifiers.

## Candidate 04

Path: `candidate_04.png`

Status: candidate, not selected.

Strengths:

- Strongest official female Byleth costume read so far: white collar, gold chest crest, pink cord, black cropped outfit, lace tights, tall boots, cape tails, bracers, and Sword of the Creator silhouette.
- Cleaner skin and refined film lighting are closer to the updated visual target.
- Full-body framing makes it useful for costume review and character-sheet comparison.

Issues:

- Reads slightly more like combat key art than a quiet professor dialogue portrait.
- Sword shape is approximate and should be inspected before final combat use.
- The cropped outfit is faithful to female Byleth, but may need a less revealing dialogue variant for some scenes.

Recommendation:

Prefer candidate 04 as the current official-fidelity Byleth direction. Keep candidate 03 as the cleaner subdued professor portrait reference.
