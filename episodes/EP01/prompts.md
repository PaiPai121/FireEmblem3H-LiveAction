# EP01 生成提示词 - 梦境初遇

## 已选参考建议

- 苏谛斯优先候选：`assets/characters/Sothis/EP01_candidate_03.png` 或 `assets/characters/Sothis/EP01_candidate_04.png`
- 贝雷丝 / 贝雷特优先候选：`assets/characters/Byleth/EP01_candidate_04.png`
- 场景：`assets/locations/DreamSanctuary/EP01_梦境空间提示词.md`
- 场景参考图：`assets/locations/DreamSanctuary/EP01_scene_reference.png`

## 通用风格

Live-action cinematic dark dream sanctuary, ultra realistic, quiet divine atmosphere, ancient floating stone platform, ancient stone throne, endless black void, subtle green particles drifting in the air, soft mysterious light, minimal movement, restrained performance, no game UI, no anime, no exaggerated magic effects.

Negative prompt:

anime, cartoon, chibi, game UI, logo, text, watermark, bright fantasy explosion, colorful magic beams, modern room, sci-fi spaceship, overacting, distorted face, bad hands, extra limbs, sexualized styling, horror gore

## Shot001

Video prompt:

Ultra wide live-action cinematic shot inside a dark dream sanctuary. An ancient stone platform floats in an endless black void. At the center is an old stone throne. Sothis, a mysterious youthful green-haired girl with an ornate gold headpiece and blue-purple-gold robe, sits quietly on the throne. Subtle green particles drift through the air. Slow push in. Her hair and robe move gently as if in weightless air. Almost no action. Quiet divine atmosphere.

中文判断：必须先让观众看懂“这不是现实”。空间要黑、空、无边界；绿色粒子只要一点点。

## Shot002

Video prompt:

Medium close live-action shot of Sothis seated in the dream sanctuary. She slowly raises her head and looks toward the camera for the first time, as if sensing someone entering the dream. Her expression is calm with slight confusion. Soft shallow depth of field, slow push in, ancient stone throne behind her, subtle green particles floating.

中文判断：动作只有“抬头”和“看向主角”，不要夸张。

## Shot003

Video prompt:

Close-up live-action shot of Sothis in the dark dream sanctuary. She looks directly toward the unseen protagonist with quiet curiosity, no smile. She blinks gently and speaks naturally with subtle lip movement. Mysterious soft light, green hair, ornate gold headpiece, blue and purple robe with gold details, restrained divine presence.

Dialogue:

汝在那里做什么？

中文判断：重点是口型自然和眼神好奇，不要做成可爱卖萌。

## Shot004

Video prompt:

Medium fixed live-action shot of Byleth standing silently inside the dark dream sanctuary. The protagonist faces Sothis off camera and does not speak. Dark empty void, ancient stone floor beneath their feet, subtle green particles drifting around. Slight breathing camera movement, calm and mysterious mood, restrained expression.

中文判断：主角只需要安静站着，看向苏谛斯。

## Shot005

Video prompt:

Close live-action shot returning to Sothis on the ancient stone throne. She slightly frowns, leans forward a little, and studies the unseen protagonist more seriously. Her expression shifts from confusion to focused recognition. Subtle green particles, dark dream sanctuary, soft divine light, minimal movement.

Dialogue:

呼啊……虽然不知道汝为何会来到这里……

中文判断：要有“她开始意识到对方真实存在”的变化。

## Shot006

Video prompt:

Wide two-character live-action shot in the dark dream sanctuary. Byleth stands in the foreground, seen from behind or three-quarter back, silently facing Sothis. Sothis sits on the ancient stone throne across the floating stone platform. The two figures stare at each other across the dream space. Subtle green particles drift slowly between them. Quiet divine atmosphere. The image slowly fades to black.

中文判断：这是结尾悬念镜头，空间关系要清楚，画面要安静。
